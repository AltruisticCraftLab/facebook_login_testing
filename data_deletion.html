Data Deletion Request

Your Data Privacy Rights

We respect your right to request the deletion of your personal data. In accordance with our privacy policy and data protection regulations, we provide this secure method for submitting data deletion requests.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your personal data:

  1. Send an email to: [Your Email Address]
  2. Include the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Provide your full name and email address associated with your account
  4. Specify any additional information that will help us identify your data

Our Process

Upon receiving your request, we will:

  1. Verify your identity to ensure the security of your data
  2. Process your deletion request within 14 business days
  3. Send you a confirmation email once the deletion is complete
  4. Provide a detailed report of what data was deleted
